^XCOMMANDS^.^N
LIST                    : list your characters
[CHARACTER NAME]        : log in as that character
RETIRE [CHARACTER NAME] : delete one of your characters
NEW [CHARACTER NAME]    : create a new character
IMPORT [CHARACTER NAME] : import your unlisted characters
PASSWORD                : change your account password
ANSI [ON/OFF]           : toggle ansi color
EMAIL                   : change your account email address
QUIT                    : log out of the system
